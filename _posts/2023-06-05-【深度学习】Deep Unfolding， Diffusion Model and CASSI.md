# Deep Unfolding

病态逆问题通常求解下式：

$$
y = \Phi x + n
$$

从贝叶斯视角，可以是用最大后验估计，求解上式

$$
\hat x = \text{arg} \max_x p(x \mid y) = \text{arg} \max_x \frac{p(y \mid x) p(x)}{p(y)} = \text{arg} \max_x p(y \mid x)p(x)
$$
