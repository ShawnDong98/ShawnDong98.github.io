# 标准四段十三句

## 第一段: 总结， 引出总论点

### 第一句， 用另一种表述方式转述考试题目

**art & tech**

In the age of X1 is thriving rapidly everyday and incredible supports for xxx are deniable, X2 is still no less important when people rate the value of X1 very high.

**anti-social behavior**

In the age of social is developing rapidly everyday, the widespread problem of X towards others has long been a major topic of concern in society.



### 第二句， 我的总论点（支持，反对......）

**art & tech**

Indeed, there are some affects from ... to soul of human that ... never can do.


**anti-social behavior**

Some of major culprits of this problem will be discussed before the most important solutions are drawn.

## 第二段： 抛出分论点一， 论证

### 第三句， 提出分论点1

**art & tech**

One thing X1 teach us where X2 is silent is the appreciation of life.

**anti-social behavior**

People may hold an anti-social attitude or disrespect others because their living environment. 


### 第四句，第五句 阐述分论点1（展开）

**art & tech**

We are surrounded by nature which is rare combination of science and arts.

If we leave out the art and beauty from nature, it would become a dull theory of how molecules work together.

**aiti-social behavior**

Living in an enviroment where violence and discrimination are the norms is severely harmful to a child.

### 第六句， 举例支持分论点1

**art & tech**

To give an example, if we listen to the music of Mozart, we can learn about harmony and joy through the medium of music or if we read classical novels, the past world reveals itself to us. 

**anti-social behavior**

To give an example, children of sexist parents in rural families in Vietnan, irrespective of their gender, show a worring tendency of disrespecting women, or children of  busy parents who are indifferent to childhood education show indifferent in others too.

### 第七句， 总结， 分论点1成立

**art & tech**

Thus, when we look back at the stars in a dark night, we appreciate the beauty and art have taught us to appreciate this beauty.

**anti-social behavior**

Thus, when there is no none to help young children distinguish between the good and the bad, ill-mannered attitudes may emerge and develop among them without being controlled.

## 第三段： 抛出分论点二， 论证

### 第八句 提出分论点2

**art & tech**

The great artists tell us about the true joys of life. 

**anti-social behaviour**

### 第九句， 阐述分论点2（展开）

**art & tech**

A classic book can teach us more about life than a biology book can.

**anti-social behaviour**

### 第十句， 举例支持分论点2

**art & tech**

To give an example, We do not need understand arts to appreciate music, books or art exhibition but we need to understand scientfic theories to understand how something really works.

**anti-social behaviour**

### 第十一句， 总结， 分论点2成立

**art & tech**

Thus,  the artists reflect life and teach us to learn about our inner-value.

**anti-social behaviour**

## 第四段： 总结论点， 首尾呼应

### 第十二句， 总论点成立

**art & tech**

So to say, science and technology have made our physical world more comfortable in this century and will keep bringing new wonders in our daily life, arts and humanities have taught us to appreciate life, to live for a reason.

**anti-social behaviour**
